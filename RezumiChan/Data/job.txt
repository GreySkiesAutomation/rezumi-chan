Senior Hardware Engineer - Robotics R&D

Austin, Texas

Unrivalled Package + Equity


Our client, a leading Austin-based technology company is looking for an experienced Senior R&D Hardware Engineer. This permanent role offers a hybrid work schedule with an immediate start.


Role Overview:


    Spearhead the development of an advanced multi-finger robotic hand through multiple fast-paced design iterations.
    Engage in high-level decisions concerning the architecture and design of the robotic hand.
    Apply analytical approaches, such as mathematical modeling and simulations, to design, refine, and optimize cutting-edge robotic hands.
    Oversee the entire process from concept creation, CAD modeling, analysis, documentation, to validation, ensuring technical viability and market readiness.
    Develop and test mechanical subsystems of the hand to evaluate the feasibility of concepts.


Skills and Qualifications:


    Proficient in 3D CAD software for creating intricate part geometries.
    Experienced in designing and constructing prototype hardware.
    Skilled in simulation and analysis tools (e.g., FEA, MATLAB).
    Knowledgeable in miniature actuators and drivetrains, both linear and rotary.
    Familiar with fingertip sensing technologies.
    Adept at integrating electromechanical components, including sensors, motors, motor drivers, wiring, and communication systems.
    Experienced in designing complex, highly articulated robotic systems.
    Well-versed in the latest developments in dexterous robotic manipulation.


Education and Experience:


    Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Mechanical, Biomechanical, Robotics, Mechatronics, or a related field.
    Over 5 years of experience in Mechanical Engineering, with a focus on solving intricate electro-mechanical challenges.
    Proven track record of delivering sophisticated electromechanical systems, such as robots, industrial automation equipment, or related systems combining actuation, motion control, custom sensor integration, and electronics.
    Experience in rapid prototyping and fabrication techniques, including CNC/manual machining, welding, laser cutting, and additive manufacturing.
